## What the Assessment Center Does
The Lorenz ISTA Assessment Center is a TESDA-authorized facility where both LISTA graduates AND walk-in candidates from other schools, companies, or workplaces can have their competencies formally evaluated and certified. Passing an assessment results in a National Certificate (NC I, NC II, or NC III) — a nationally and internationally recognized qualification.

## 3-Step Assessment Process
1. **Register** — Contact LISTA Assessment Center, submit application form and required documents, pay assessment fee.
